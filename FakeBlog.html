Rachel Gonzalez - Violinist

Rachel Gonzalez is a violin player based in the Chicago area. She is a college graduate from the University of Colorado with a degree in Music Industry studies. She has appeared on stage and on recordings with many Chicago bands such as Charlie Reed, Parent, Glad Rags, Dissonant Desert and more. She is a member of the Lakeview Orchestra and gives violin lessons in the Chicago area.

For inquiries regarding recording or violin lessons, please send me an email.
